---
title: "Choose the right org type"
description: "Choose the right organization type: `Developer` or `Merchant`."
---

There are two types of organizations in Octogen: `Developer` and `Merchant`.

- `Developer`: API access to structured and enriched public product data across any and all sites.

- `Merchant`: Merchant organizations are scoped for one or more catalogs. They can:
   - Steer Octogen's autonomous "catalog enrichment" system.
   - Publish their enriched catalog data to other systems. Eg: as `.md` routes for LLMs.
   - Integrate semantic search, and other catalog-related features into their own products and services.
